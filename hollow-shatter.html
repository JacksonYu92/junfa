Hollow Shatter Glass

Introduction
Hollow Shatter Glass Window is a traditional sunshade product which is typically opened or closed by using the manual pulling cords or mechanical methods. Relying on advanced technology , the blinds are in stalled in the hollow glass and then use the magnetic force to control the blinds in the insulating glass to get it easily lifted or turned 180 degrees. This product is not only save the space but also shade the sunshine as well as keeping the temperature and proof the noise, meanwhile, it brings a new view for the in door environmerit of the building. No matter it`s in summer or winter, the adjusted blinds can be used to shade the sunshine or get the sunshine. This will save the energy from the air-conditioning. According the calculation, when the blinds is closed, the energy saving rate can reach 40%. And because of the double layers of tempered glass structure, the resistance for wind and external force is higher, which is applied for the buildings along the sea or very high buildings. This product is also the substitute of the traditional proof wall. Hollow shatter glass window is a new product which install the blinds in side the glass, the blinds can be adjusted in any angle for full-transmission , semi-transparent or shade. It can also be pulled up and become a lighttransmission window . It is specially showed the unique temperature preserve, sound proof, anti-dust,anti-pollution and safety features, which is the ideal product of the sunlight shade . The product has reasonable using space : no need specific space for the blinds installation which make more space . Sound proof effect is better: through the wider hollow isolation to create a quite environment to strengthen the ability of sound-proof , heat-isolating, keep warm, wind protect!on. Cleaning environment : isolating the dust and pollution particles to create the comfortable living environment . Economical features : no need to worry for the damages ,  and can be cleaned and used for a long time.



Product Feature

Energy Saving : Hollow shatter glass is made of high-quality double-layer tempered glass composite built-in imported aluminum louver and single-handie magnetron control unit . In summer, the blind can be adjusted to close status to block the sunshine and isolate the hot and cold air circulation which will greatly reduce the energy loss . In win ter, can pull up the blinds a nd let the s unshine in and absor b the heat along with the 20mm hollow isolation to keep the indoor temperature , and thereby to save energy and expenses . Hollow blind glass is a new energy-saving product in which blinds are mounted in hollow glass . Blinds are traditional heat insulation and shade products . Insulating glass is a heat preservation product formed in recent years . By integrating the traditional blinds and hollow glass into one body , and realizes operation of blinds through magnetically controlled device and lifting device , to save space and also give glass doors and windows good shading performance , improve insulation performance of the insulating glass and in door light environmerit .

Dust-Proof, Anti-Oil and Pollution-Proof : the product has the advantages of dustproof, fume-proof and anti-pollution. At the same time , due to the use of high-tech magnetic induction transmission system , the movement of closing and opening , up and down is more convenient ; the blinds doesn` t need not be cleaned which can save time , effort , and money . Built-in blinds Insulating glass door and window system creates a personalized and private space which brings you unconsciously a quiet , comfortable and healthy environment.

Fire Proof : In any building , the traditional curtains are flammable, and once there is a fire incidents, the curtains made of cloth, chemical fiber and other materials will release a large amount of toxic smoke which will cause suffocation and casualties to the room personnel . The built-in blinds hollow glass is not only exposed to open flames , but also not release heavy smoke in the fire . Double-layer tempered glass and built-in aluminum-magnesium shutters also have the function of blocking the flame transmission , which will effectively reduce the probability of fire occurrence and being a reliable fireproof product.

Sound Proof : As tested , hollow glass can generally reduce the noise transmitted to the room around 25dB , while the single-handle magnetron built-in blinds insulating glass will reduce 36dB of the noise in the room , ensuring a perfect sound insulation effect.

Anti-Frost : In the places with a very cold winter , the temperature differenee between indoor and outdoor is very large , so the grass window and doors are usually will have frost. By using of built-in blinds hollow glass door and window system will stop the water and humidity leakage due to its good air tightness and water tightness, which will also effectively avoid the phenomenon of frost freezing on the door and window glass.
