The Latest Generation of Convection Double-Chamber Tempering Furnace Settles in at Jiangmen Junfa Safety Glass Co., Ltd.

November is a season of abundance; just as Jack Ma created the legend of "Double 11", Junfa Glass also welcomed another significant addition in this month – the new generation Hangzhou JINGGONG double-chamber convection tempering furnace.

Introducing the JINGGONG Double-Chamber Convection Tempering Furnace

November is a season of abundance; as Jack Ma created the legend of "Double 11", Junfa Glass also introduced another significant addition in this month – the new generation Hangzhou JINGGONG double-chamber convection tempering furnace.

With a commitment to achieving high customer satisfaction, Junfa's mission for service, and in order to further enhance product quality while shortening supply cycles, we constantly improve our process and introduce new equipment. Through the combined diligent efforts of Junfa's technical experts and JINGGONG's senior engineers, the installation, commissioning, and production of the double-chamber tempering furnace were completed ahead of schedule. The preheating and heating sections seamlessly align, significantly enhancing the visual quality of physically tempered glass. This new facility is now fully operational, serving our wide range of customers!

Junfa aims to go beyond the realm of "images for reference only" and bring the effect of glass curtain walls to life faster through dedicated efforts!

Equipment Specifications:

  • Capable of processing 4~19mm glass
  • Maximum processing dimensions for single chamber: 2700*5000mm
  • Minimum processing dimensions: 300*300mm

Its key features include:

  • The upper furnace body employs a convection heating system utilizing JINGGONG's patented technology. Heating units are arranged in a matrix pattern, with each unit made of high-grade, high-temperature resistant stainless steel.
  • Heating elements within the convection heating units are made of nickel-chromium alloy, ensuring durability and positioned within the heating units.
  • The lower furnace body utilizes radiant heating technology, with heating elements made of iron-chromium-aluminum material. Ceramic blocks are employed for heating element installation, connected with high-temperature soldering.

PS: Official data on the double-chamber furnace evaluation as follows:

Regular glass tempering furnaces encounter significant optical deformities (wavy lines), curvature of thin glass, pinprick defects on thick glass, white haze, and furnace explosions when producing tempered architectural glass, especially laminated and coated glass. However, these issues are effectively addressed within the double-chamber glass tempering furnace.

User-friendly operation, convenient maintenance, and stable product quality.

We extend our heartfelt gratitude for the steadfast support of Junfa Glass. "With this remarkable addition, worry-free production is at hand!" We warmly welcome both new and existing customers to place orders.
