Projected Compound Annual Growth Rate of 4.8% for Global Energy-Efficient Glass Market in the Coming Years

Projected Compound Annual Growth Rate of 4.8% for Global Energy-Efficient Glass Market in the Coming Years

  • China Glass Network
  • Feb 15, 2020

The global energy-efficient glass market is anticipated to grow by $9.5 billion in the next few years, with a compound annual growth rate of 4.8%. Online coated glass is expected to exhibit growth potential exceeding 5.6%, as the driving force behind this evolving trend of growth necessitates that businesses keep up with the ever-changing market dynamics. Notably, Kaphs Company is at the forefront of this.

Online coated glass is projected to reach over $13.3 billion by 2025, promising substantial returns and adding significant impetus to global growth.

Representing developed countries, the United States is set to maintain a growth momentum of 3.7%. Europe continues to play a crucial role in the global economy.

Over the next 5 to 6 years, Germany is poised to contribute an additional $333.1 million in size and influence to the region.

Anticipated demand exceeding $267.4 million will stem from other European markets. By the end of the forecast period, Japan's online coated glass market size is expected to reach $613.6 million. As the world's second-largest economy and a new game-changer in the global market landscape, China is projected to achieve a growth rate of 7.7% in the coming years, bringing about potential business opportunities worth around $2.8 billion.

Several macroeconomic factors and internal market forces will determine the growth and development of demand patterns in emerging countries in the Asia-Pacific, Latin America, and Middle East regions.

Key players in the energy-efficient glass market include Abrisa Technologies, Bendheim, Central Glass, China Southern Glass (CSG), Fuso Glass India, GSC Glass, Jadrian Glass, Kaphs Company, Morley Glass, Nippon Sheet Glass (NSG), Saint-Gobain, Schott, Sedak Company, SISECAM Group, Taiwan Glass, and Vitro Glass.
